World's Best Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Ingredients For Making porkchops

A quick and simple grilled pork chop that everyone will love featuring a simple and easy glaze.

  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 2 ⅔ tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 (4 ounce) (1-inch thick) pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Procedure

  • Step 1 Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  • Step 2 Whisk ketchup, honey, soy sauce, and garlic together in a bowl to make a glaze.
  • Step 3 Sear the pork chops on both sides on the preheated grill. Lightly brush glaze onto each side of the chops as they cook;
  • Step 4 Grill until no longer pink in the center, about 7 to 9 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
